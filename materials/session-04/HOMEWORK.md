# Homework 04 — Planetary Safety Scanner

Ask for:

- planet name;
- danger level 1–5;
- oxygen answer (`yes` or `no`).

Display one of:

- safe to explore;
- proceed with caution;
- exploration cancelled.

Use at least one `if`, one `elif`, and one `else`.

Submit:

- code;
- answer: “Which rule was hardest to design, and why?”

Optional: temperature, password clearance, or emergency mode.
