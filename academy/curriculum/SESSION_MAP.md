`print()`, strings, order; Speech Module; startup sequence; First Contact.

Variables, strings, integers, arithmetic; Memory Core; robot profile; Memory Engineer.

## 3 — Open the Communication Channel
`input()`, `int()`, f-strings; Communication Channel; crew interview; Signal Operator.

## 4 — Activate the Decision Core
`if`, `elif`, `else`, comparisons; Decision Core; launch and safety rules; Logic Navigator.

## 5 — Repeat Protocol
`for`, `range()`; countdown and rescue signal; Loop Master.

## 6 — Navigation Scanner
Loops, conditions, counters; sector scan; Star Chart Navigator.

## 7 — Cargo Bay
Lists; cargo management; Cargo Master.

## 8 — Skill Modules
Functions, parameters, returns; reusable abilities; Module Architect.

## 9 — Planetary Database
Dictionaries; survey data; Planet Surveyor.

## 10 — Random Space Events
`random`, state change; event engine; Event Handler.

## 11 — Launch Assembly
Integration, refactoring, testing; Launch Commander.

## 12 — Mission to Andromeda
Final integrated project and presentation; Andromeda Explorer.
